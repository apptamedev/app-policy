Privacy Policy for AppTame

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

Thank you for choosing AppTame ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within our application. Our core principle is simple: your data is yours, and it stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

AppTame is built from the ground up with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or other contact information.
  • The list of applications you select to manage.
  • Your configured schedules or screen time settings.
  • Your device usage data.
  • Any payment information.

All data generated by you within the app is stored exclusively on your local device and, if you enable it, synced to your personal iCloud account.

How We Use Apple's Screen Time Frameworks

AppTame utilizes Apple's native frameworks to help you manage screen time. These include:

  • FamilyControls & ManagedSettings: To allow you to select applications and set rules.
  • DeviceActivity: To monitor usage and enforce the schedules you create.

All processing and monitoring performed using these frameworks happen entirely on your device. The information never leaves your phone and is never shared with us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

AppTame offers premium features via in-app purchases. All transactions are processed securely by the Apple App Store. We do not collect, receive, or store any payment details. Your purchase information is managed directly by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policies and terms.

No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

AppTame does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your activity within the app is not monitored or analyzed by us or anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be used by parents to manage their children's screen time. We do not collect any personal information from children. All configuration and usage data remains on the user's device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy within the app and on our website. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
